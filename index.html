🚧 The Problem

Residents and government teams in Abu Dhabi face fragmented, slow, and manual processes for reporting and resolving public infrastructure issues:

  • Siloed reporting channels across different entities (Municipality, RTA, Utilities)
  • Limited AI automation — manual classification and routing delays fixes
  • No predictive insights — reactive repairs increase costs and citizen frustration

🤖 The Solution: Baladi AI

Baladi AI is a next‑generation, AI‑powered platform that enables anyone to report public infrastructure issues using a photo, video, voice note, or message — in any language — and have the AI automatically classify, enrich, and route the report to the exact responsible department for fast resolution.

Key Features:

  • 📸 One‑tap photo/video reporting with AI classification
  • 🎤 Voice‑first multilingual chatbot (Arabic, English, Hindi, Urdu, etc.)
  • 🧠 Agentic AI workflows per government department
  • 🔮 Predictive maintenance & infrastructure health scores
  • 🌐 Digital twin integration for real‑time urban insights

⚙️ How It Works

  1. Report: Citizen captures a photo/video or voice note describing the issue.
  2. AI Triage: AI classifies the issue, enriches metadata, and routes to the correct department.
  3. Fix: Field team receives AI‑pre‑filled work order, completes repair, and uploads “fixed” photo.
  4. Verify: AI verifies the fix and notifies the citizen for confirmation.
